Small group

Small Group has many advantages. Comfort in numbers seems to be the winner. Studies show attendance and work capacity increase when working out with a friend or group. The shared financial commitment is much more favorable than that of a 1-on-1 option. The trainers ability to scale workouts in groups of 3-4 is far more advantageous over larger group fitness classes. Personal attention is very much served throughout the entire Small Group Training session
